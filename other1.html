About Us

Mission Awareness Foundation (MAF) is a non-profit societal organisation. It was established in 2020 by a group of well educated and socially responsible personalities. MAF carries out various developmental programs to uplift the socially, economically and educationally marginalized communities. The organisation continues to work for the elimination of child labor, educational empowerment, the empowerment of women, youth, and the comprehensive development of the community.
Our Work
MISSION

To improve the quality of life of the marginalized sectors of society by influencing individuals, groups and institutions to realize their humanitarian concern; and by providing a venue for them to contribute their resources and succeed in a rapidly changing society.

VISION

The Vision of MAF is to become a catalyst for progression, enhancement and enrichment of the society through awareness in the field of Education, Health, Sports and Skill Development programs.
