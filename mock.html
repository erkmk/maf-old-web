Free Mock Test

The ongoing COVID-19 pandemic in India has resulted in the cancellation of 10th board exams and for the 1st time in its history, the government has decided to take a common Entrance Exam for students seeking admission in 11th STD. All the candidates who have passed SSC Class 10 are eligible to take part in FYJC Common Entrance Test. The Maharashtra education board will conduct CET on 21 Aug 2021.

Mission Awareness Foundation has decided to conduct Mock Exam on 5 th August 2021 for helping students adapt to the latest mode of examinations. Registration & participation are free of cost and the main objective of the mock Test is to get maximum practice out of the students, helping them get top scores in the final exam for admission into the best colleges of Maharashtra.

Key Features of the mock Exam:

1. Mock Test is completely free for the students.

2. Question papers are prepared for English, Semi-English & Urdu medium students.

3. Papers are prepared according to the Latest Pattern & Syllabus defined by the board.

4. Papers are prepared by a team of Expert teachers with respective the subjects.

5. Students can collect hard copy of question papers from various centers.

6. Detailed Analysis & Solutions will be provided to the students.

7. OMR sheets of all students will be checked and Ranks will be given.
